隐私政策 / Privacy Policy — 生效日期 Effective: 2026-02-02

一、我们是谁

《不干了》（以下简称“本应用”）是一款用于情绪记录与表达的轻量级工具，旨在帮助用户进行自我情绪确认与个人反思。我们尊重用户隐私，并遵循最小必要原则处理数据。

二、我们收集哪些信息

1. 我们不收集的内容

本应用不会收集以下任何信息：

  • 姓名、手机号、邮箱等个人身份信息
  • 身份证号、精确地理位置、通讯录
  • 公司名称、雇主信息、具体工作单位
  • 账号信息（本应用不提供注册或登录功能）

2. 本地生成的数据

以下数据仅保存在用户设备本地：

  • 情绪点击记录
  • 使用天数与连续签到状态
  • 勋章解锁状态

这些数据不会与服务器同步，也不会用于用户识别。

3. 匿名统计数据

在用户主动选择所在行业后，本应用可能会匿名上传以下信息，仅用于群体统计分析：

  • 用户选择的行业分类（非具体公司）
  • 与行业相关的匿名情绪选择结果
  • 时间戳（不包含具体行为轨迹）

重要说明：

  • 不包含用户身份信息
  • 不包含设备标识符（如 IDFA、IMEI）
  • 不建立用户画像
  • 数据仅用于生成个人签到报告或聚合统计结果

三、数据的使用方式

我们收集的匿名统计数据仅用于：

  • 生成群体层面的行业情绪统计
  • 展示去标识化的趋势信息（如行业排名）

我们不会将数据用于广告投放、用户画像或第三方商业用途。

四、分享功能说明

本应用支持将生成的情绪状态卡片以图片形式分享至第三方平台（如微信）。

  • 分享行为由用户主动触发；
  • 分享内容为图片，不包含可追踪链接；
  • 本应用不会追踪或分析分享后的行为；

第三方平台的使用行为将受其各自隐私政策约束。

五、第三方服务

本应用目前不接入第三方广告或追踪服务。如未来引入新的第三方服务，我们将更新本隐私政策并明确告知用户。

六、数据安全

我们采取合理的技术与管理措施，确保匿名统计数据仅用于聚合分析，避免被用于个人识别。

七、儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会有意收集任何未成年人的个人信息。

八、隐私政策的变更

如本隐私政策发生变更，我们将通过应用内提示或相关页面进行更新说明。

1. Who we are

Not Today (hereinafter "the App") is a lightweight tool for recording and expressing emotions, designed to help users confirm their feelings and reflect personally. We respect user privacy and handle data according to the principle of data minimization.

2. What information we collect

1. Information we do NOT collect

The App does NOT collect any of the following information:

  • Names, phone numbers, email addresses, or other personal identifiers
  • ID numbers, precise geo-location, or address book/contacts
  • Company name, employer details, or specific workplace information
  • Account credentials (the App does not provide registration or login)

2. Locally generated data

The following data is stored only locally on the user's device:

  • Emotion tap/click records
  • Usage days and consecutive check-in status
  • Badge/unlock states

These data are not synchronized to any server and are not used to identify users.

3. Anonymous aggregated data

If a user voluntarily selects an industry category, the App may anonymously upload the following information for group-level statistical analysis only:

  • Selected industry category (not specific company)
  • Anonymous emotion choices related to the industry
  • Timestamps (not detailed behavioral traces)

Important:

  • No user identity information is included
  • No device identifiers (e.g., IDFA, IMEI) are included
  • No user profiling is performed
  • Data are used only to produce check-in reports or aggregated statistics

3. How we use the data

Anonymous aggregated data is used only to:

  • Generate industry-level emotion statistics
  • Show de-identified trend information (e.g., industry rankings)

We will NOT use data for advertising, user profiling, or third-party commercial purposes.

4. Sharing / Share feature

The App allows users to share generated emotion cards as images to third-party platforms (e.g., WeChat).

  • Sharing is initiated by the user;
  • Shared content is an image and does not contain trackable links;
  • The App does not track or analyze behavior after sharing;

Third-party platforms are governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Third-party services

The App currently does not integrate third-party ads or tracking services. If we add new third-party services in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify users.

6. Data security

We apply reasonable technical and administrative measures to ensure anonymous aggregated data is used only for aggregation and cannot be used for personal identification.

7. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

8. Changes to this policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, we will update the information in-app or on the relevant page.
